# Welcome to My CTF Writeups Blog

Hello and welcome!

I'm **3l3v3n**, a cybersecurity student with a growing interest in **Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR)** and **defensive security**.

I created this blog to document my learning journey through Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, security challenges, and hands-on labs. As I solve challenges, I'll share detailed writeups explaining my thought process, methodologies, tools, and solutions.

## What You'll Find Here

* CTF challenge writeups
* DFIR and blue-team related challenges
* Security tips and tricks
* Useful tools and techniques
* Notes from my learning journey

## Why This Blog?

One of the best ways to learn cybersecurity is by documenting what you discover. Writing solutions not only helps reinforce concepts but also creates a valuable knowledge base for future reference.

My goal is to share practical content that may help other students, aspiring security professionals, and CTF enthusiasts learn something new.

## Disclaimer

All content published on this blog is intended for educational and ethical purposes only. Any techniques discussed should only be used in authorized environments.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find these writeups useful.

Happy hacking! 🚩
